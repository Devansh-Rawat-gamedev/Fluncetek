OUR TECHNOLOGIES
SOPRANO TITANIUM LASER

The Soprano Titanium Laser is excellent for removing unwanted hair. Its innovative 3D technology combines the three most efficient hair removal laser wavelengths (755nm, 810nm, and 1064nm) into a single applicator. It improves the skin's quality, smoothes it, and removes dryness and ingrown hairs. The most widely utilized lasers by laser professionals can eradicate pimples, irritations, and even the slightest skin imperfections.

HYDRAFACIAL MD

HydraFacial MD refers to an exclusive variety of boosters and chemical peel agents. It uses the most advanced technology of powerful diamond-head microdermabrasion to gently massage, clean, and exfoliate skin. It offers a wide range of benefits, such as deep cleaning, acne, scar removal, wrinkle reduction, skin care, hair regrowth, etc. This is great for all skin types; that’s what sets HydraFacials apart from other facials.

Harmony XL Pro

Harmony XL PRO provides a comprehensive set of technologies that allow ablative and non-ablative treatments as well as unique combinations for a wide range of indications. It is used to address the aesthetic concerns of patients of all ages and skin types. It is used to treat skin resurfacing, tone and texture, skin remodeling and lifting, pigmented lesions and tattoos, hair removal, acne and acne scars, and other issues.

CoolSculpting®

CoolSculpting® is a popular non-invasive fat-reduction procedure. It is the only fat-freezing technology that has received the coveted US FDA approval. This method involves freezing fat cells and removing them from the body naturally and automatically. The procedure targets areas of stubborn fat that are resistant to diet and exercise. It is designed to target fat cells on the inner and outer thighs, abdomen, sides, upper arms, and chin.
